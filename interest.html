Reading

According to one statistic, the average Malaysian spending time reading books is still at an unsatisfactory level. This is because the interest in reading does not apply when they are young until they climb to adulthood. There are many factors that cause such problems, including themselves, their parents, financial constraints, and more. In order to prevent this from prolonging, the government has launched an initiative of reading-enhancing program, the "10 Minutes Read Together Program" on April 23 every year. The aim of this program is nothing else, not to nurture the spirit of reading in the Malaysian people's community so that the interest in reading books even for 10 minutes.

Returning back to my childhood, I'm, honestly, not very interested in reading any kind of writing. But the newspapers are something I've always been interested in reading because the papers at the time had a mind-challenging game, so I was tempted to read them while finishing the game. If you want to compare the past with the present, the content of the newspapers no longer attracts my attention to read it, but not to not read it directly, just to spend time.

In addition to reading newspapers, reading comics especially digital comics is also a hobby that is done almost all the time. The existence of a comic book reading platform has been a little as boosting my interest in spending time reading. There's no number of comic book readings because there's a lot of titles and story paths that draw attention to reading them. There are two reading platforms that I always use, namely:

  1. MangaDex
